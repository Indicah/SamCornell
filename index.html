Samuel Cornell

about

Samuel was singing before he could speak and singing with legible words at two as well. When he was three, he began to ask if he could sing at church. His gracious Sunday School teacher allowed him to do so and the children and teachers alike just loved it. He gave his heart to the Lord at four years old and shortly thereafter began to write songs that the Lord would give him.
